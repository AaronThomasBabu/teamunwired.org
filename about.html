About Teamunwired

Hailing from God’s own country, Team Unwired is an ensemble of undergraduates pursuing engineering as their major at the National Institute of Technology Calicut (NITC), the country’s best technical institute. Team Unwired strives towards achieving its primary goal to gain hands on experience of building an engineering prototype from the knowledge and concepts gained over the course of the academic years.

Team Unwired is the forefront of technical projects in NITC, being involved in a wide array of activities and events nationwide, competing to the very top at every opportunity. Events ranging in areas of aero modelling, radio control modelling, rocket modelling, website designing, and its most important field automobiles. Team Unwired also holds regular CAD and auto seminars for free of admission available to anyone willing to join the sessions for the simple cause of spreading knowledge and encouraging students to take up innovative projects. Ensuring hands on learning which supplements classroom learning, strengthens fundamentals and cultivates skills that will help students further their career objectives.

Started only three short years ago by three motivated students, the achievements is a long list that the ever growing team since then is to be proud of.
