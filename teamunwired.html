The start of it all

Teamunwired

Who we are:

  • A group of motivated students from NIT Calicut brought together by their common love for technology.
  • From 2nd to 4th year students across various disciplines.
  • We are 22 in number, with division of labour for efficient functioning.

What we do:

  • We conduct scientific exploration purely to understand in depth the beauty of how the world works.
  • We undertake engineering projects that challenge us, make us think and broaden our spectrum of activities.
  • With the knowledge we gain, we participate in events all over the country.

Why we do this:

  • Because we believe challenges only make us stronger.
  • Because we strive to enhance our abilities.
  • Because we rejoice in our work.

Why we need your help:

  • Because any concrete venture needs both monetary backup and goodwill.
  • We have taken up the extremely challenging Baja SAE as our next milestone.

Activities

Aero

With a zeal to fly higher, we started with a HAB ( Hot Air Balloon) workshop which gave way to small RC planes finally leading to our very own quadropter.

In the past few years, Team-Unwired has participated in many aero-modeling competitions and organized workshops like chuck gliders to nurture young minds.

We have built three remote controlled airplanes of our own, which have flown in various college technological fests like IITBombay's Techfest 2008 (18th position out of 120+ teams)

Activities

Astro

A telescope and an open cloudless sky is all we need to explore the hidden secrets of the universe.

Seeing it through your own telescope after meticulous adjustments and calibrations however is what is truly out of the world.

We have two 90 mm Galilean telescopes and to involve our peer, star gazing and astrophotography nights are organised regularly.

Activities

Auto

We didnt let go of the love every child has for automobile. We thrive on it.

We have held exhibitions on cars ( called Wheels in Tathva) and hope we got younger minds to keep their passion for cars alive

This is not mentioning the fact that we have already built both remote controlled and life size off road vahicles

Baja SAE is our latest and most challenging project.

Activities

CAD

In todays digital world, everything is made and tested virtually. There is no wastage of material or precious time.

Our seniors took time out from their busy schedules to give us coaching on Solidworks DS. It is only with this tool that we were able to clear Virtual BAJA.

In Aakruti, a CAD competition held by dassault systems, we were the first runner up, having presented the gearbox we designed and created for our previous BAJA project.

We have used CNC machines to fabricate a VAWT after design through Solidworks.

Activities

RC Controlled planes and boats

Books can be found piled up and dog-eared, complex equations broken and our own little theories cropping up as we build remote controlled aircrafts and amphibian transporters.

In IIST's technological fest( Conscientia), we got the second and third prize for the amphibian transporter event(Salilsthalchar) and the robotics competition(Bots up).
In NIT Trichy, we were first in the “NFS Aqua” remote controlled boat event.
we won the fourth prize in IIT Bombay's 2011 robo-polo.
We have built three remote controlled airplanes of our own, which have flown in various college technological fests like IITBombay's Techfest 2008 (18th position out of 120+ teams)

Activities

Amateur rocketry

In rocketry, we conduct workshops for NIT first year students and compete in various technological festivals

We won the runners-up in bottle rocketry competition (Prakshepan) at IIST trivandrum.

Activities

Web / Paathshaala

Paathshaala started with the need for sharing a single video file.
The potential of having a website dedicated to the sharing of information among fellow students appealed to us, not just in the altruistic manner but also since it would help learn the ways of the web

Paathshaala is a place to learn and share information.It is a content sharing repository inside NIT Calicut.
This project won numerous awards including:

  • A national database design competition by Linux for You magazine.
  • Software presentation contest, login 2011, pgstech
Visit Paathshaala inside NITC Campus at http://192.168.5.27/.
