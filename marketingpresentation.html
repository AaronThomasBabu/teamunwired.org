Baja SAE:

  • It is an offroad racing event conducted around the world providing undergraduate B.Tech students with a challenging project that involves design, engineering, planning, manufacturing and marketing.
  • The 2012 Baja SAE in the Indian zone will be held in Pithampur, Indore in the month of February.
  • Each team's goal will be to design and build a prototype of a rugged, single seat, off-road recreational vehicle intended for sale to the non-professional, weekend off-road enthusiast.
  • The India chapter will also sponsor at least the best team to participate abroad.
