NIT-C Represents Conclave

A proud moment for NIT-C as it was chosen to participate in the annual Conclave event held in NIT-Nagpur. Conclave is an event where student bodies come together to represent and discuss about the interesting and innovative projects their university has either taken part in or is currently competing in. Seshan R.V represented NIT-C and Teamunwired and bagged 3rd place in the event by discusses Teamunwired's history and vision for this year Formula Student UK - 2013. Have a look at the video.
